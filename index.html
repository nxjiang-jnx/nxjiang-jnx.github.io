Nanxiang Jiang

I am currently an undergraduate student in Computer Science at Shen Yuan Honors College, Beihang University, enrolled in a selective honors program. I am fortunate to be advised by Asst. Prof. Zhaoxin Fan and Prof. Si Liu.

My research interests focus on understanding and modeling the dynamic 3D world from images and videos. I am particularly interested in building intelligent systems that can perceive and adapt to the ever-changing physical world. Recently, I have been exploring 3D human reconstruction, image and video generation, and concept erasure toward this goal.

I was born into an artistic family in Nanjing, China, and I have a deep interest in architecture, classical music, and perceiving the 3D physical world. In my spare time, I enjoy playing the piano, origami, calligraphy, and studying symphonic conducting.
