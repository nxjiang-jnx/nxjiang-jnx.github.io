Nanxiang Jiang

I am currently an undergraduate student in Computer Science at Shen Yuan Honors College, Beihang University, enrolled in the Top Talents Program in CS. I am fortunate to be advised by Asst. Prof. Zhaoxin Fan and Prof. Si Liu.

My research interests focus on understanding and modeling the dynamic 3D world from images and videos, with the broader goal of enabling intelligent embodied agents to perceive, act, and adapt in the ever-changing physical world. Recently, I have been exploring 3D reconstruction, image and video generation, and Embodied AI toward this goal.

I was born into an artistic family in Nanjing, China, and I have a deep interest in architecture, classical music, and perceiving the 3D physical world. In my spare time, I enjoy playing the piano, origami, calligraphy, and studying symphonic conducting.
